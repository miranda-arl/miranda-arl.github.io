FoodBanks of America

Tasks:

  • - Collaborated with a team of peers to design and implemented a web application using Java, JavaScript, HTML and CSS over the course of 10 weeks
  • - Leveraged various Google Cloud Platform APIs, including App Engine
  • - Practiced industry best practices such as: contributing to open-source software using Git and GitHub, conducting code reviews, participating in distributed development, designing new components and interfaces and leading them to completion

Description:

Whether you are in need of food or are interested in volunteering at your local food bank, our site will help put you on the right path by connecting you to Foodbank resources in your local community. Our map section will help you locate your closest foodbank and provide you with its relevant information when you click on a foodbank marker. There are two ways to access this feature. When the site loads, a prompt will appear that will ask if you agree to share your location. You can either agree to share your location or use the search bar to manually input a location. If you want to help but don't have the time to spare, don't worry because we also provide you with the opportunity to directly donate to organizations committed to eradicating food insecurity.
